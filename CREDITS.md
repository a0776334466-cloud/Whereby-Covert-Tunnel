## Original Research: TorKameleon
* **Researcher:** Afonso Vilalonga
* **Topic:** Steganography in video calls for censorship circumvention.
* **Original Repository:** https://github.com/AfonsoVilalonga/TorKameleon

This repository is a community initiative to evolve this research into a deployable, robust solution that can survive transcoding by live WebRTC services (such as Whereby).
